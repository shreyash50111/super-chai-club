Our Story

Born from a passion for traditional brewing, Super Chai Club brings the authentic taste of clay-pot tea infused with premium organic jaggery. We believe in tea that nourishes the soul and respects tradition.

The Excellence

Clay Pot Brewed

Traditional "Matka" style brewing for an earthy, deep flavor profile you won't find anywhere else.

Organic Jaggery

100% Sugar-free experience using high-grade organic jaggery for a healthier, richer sweetness.

Hand-Ground Spices

Fresh cardamom, ginger, and our secret SCC spice blend, crushed fresh for every batch.
